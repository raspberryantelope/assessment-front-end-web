Sandy

Utah

Sandy is a city in the Salt Lake Metropolitan Area in Salt Lake County, Utah. With a population of nearly 100,000, it is the 6th-largest city in Utah. One of the most notable features of Sandy is its proximity to two canyons that home world-renowned ski resorts, including Alta, Brighton, and Snowbird. Residents of Sandy have, at most, a 20 minute drive from anywhere in the city to reach these canyons. While the winter sporting is popular internationally, locals are also able to take advantage of nearly 250,000 acres of wilderness land for camping, hiking, fishing, and other outdoor activities.
